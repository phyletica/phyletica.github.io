Aundrea and Randy Awarded CMB Fellowships!

Congrats to Aundrea and Randy!

Congratulations to lab members Aundrea Westfall and Randy Klabacka for winning competitive Auburn University Cellular and Molecular Biosciences Peaks of Excellence Research Fellowships for the Summer of 2017! These fellowships will pay their stipends over the summer, allowing Aundrea and Randy to focus on their research.
